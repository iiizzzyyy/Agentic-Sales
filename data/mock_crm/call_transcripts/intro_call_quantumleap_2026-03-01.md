**[1:38] Sarah:** That's a complex sale. How are you ramping new people today?

**[2:02] Sarah:** And when they struggle, how do you coach them?

**[2:25] Sarah:** It sounds like you're in that classic startup phase where the founder is still doing sales but trying to build a repeatable process. Is that fair?

**[2:48] Sarah:** What does "quickly" look like for you?

**[3:00] Sarah:** It is possible with the right system. Let me tell you what we see with similar startups. The biggest challenge is usually competitive positioning — selling against larger, more established players. Does that resonate?

**[3:45] Sarah:** That's a great message — but it needs to be muscle memory for every rep. That's exactly what our roleplay platform does. Reps practice competitive scenarios until the response is automatic. "We chose QuantumLeap because..." — they hear it from the AI prospect, and they practice their response 50 times until it's natural.

**[4:10] Alex:** That would be huge. Alicia in particular struggles with the competitive positioning. She came from channel where the partner did most of the selling.
