## Talking Points for Final Call

**If they push on price:**
"I understand CompetitorBeta came in lower. A few things to consider: our implementation is included — CompetitorBeta's quote likely doesn't include their PS fees for custom configuration. Our 90-day exit clause means you're not locked in if it doesn't work. And the custom MEDDIC work we're doing — adding Technical Validation — is something you'd pay extra for with anyone else."

**If they mention CompetitorBeta's UI:**
"UI is subjective, but let me share what our customers tell us: modern UIs often prioritize aesthetics over power. Our platform is designed by sales ops people, not designers. Every click has a purpose. And for managers, the reporting views are built for glanceable insights — you shouldn't need to click around to find what matters."

**If they're stuck between options:**
"Let me ask you this: what would make you confident in this decision? Is it more references? A deeper technical review? A pilot period? I want to make sure you have everything you need to make the right choice for DataFlow — whether that's us or someone else."
