# CompetitorAlpha Battlecard

## Overview
CompetitorAlpha is our primary competitor in the mid-market segment. They focus on price and simplicity.

## Positioning
- **Their pitch**: "Simple, affordable sales training"
- **Reality**: Basic features, limited AI, no real-time coaching

## Key Differentiators (Us vs Them)

| Feature | SalesCoach AI | CompetitorAlpha |
|---------|---------------|-----------------|
| Real-time AI coaching | ✅ Yes | ❌ No |
| CRM integration depth | Deep (bi-directional) | Basic (read-only) |
| Custom playbook ingestion | ✅ Yes | ❌ No |
| Roleplay scenarios | Unlimited, AI-generated | 50 pre-built only |
| Data residency options | US, EU, APAC | US only |
| SOC 2 Type II | ✅ Certified | ❌ Not certified |

## Common Objections & Responses

### "CompetitorAlpha is cheaper"
**Response**: "You're right that their sticker price is lower. But when you factor in the time your managers spend manually coaching without AI assistance, the total cost of ownership is actually higher. Our customers see 60% faster ramp time, which means new reps are productive sooner. Would you like to see the ROI model?"

### "We already use CompetitorAlpha"
**Response**: "That's great that you've invested in sales training. What's working well? [Listen] Many of our customers came from CompetitorAlpha because they hit a ceiling - either the scenarios got stale or they needed deeper analytics. Is that something you've experienced?"

### "Their UI is simpler"
**Response**: "Simplicity is valuable. We've designed our interface to be just as intuitive for reps while giving managers the depth they need. In fact, most users say our Slack integration is simpler than logging into a separate tool. Can I show you?"

## Landmines (Questions to ask that expose their weaknesses)
1. "How do you handle GDPR for EU employees?"
2. "Can you ingest our existing sales playbook?"
3. "What real-time feedback do reps get during actual calls?"
4. "How do you measure coaching effectiveness over time?"
