# Cold Call Opening Script

## The Pattern Interrupt Opener

**Goal**: Get 30 seconds of attention, not a meeting on the first call.

### Script Template

> "Hi [NAME], this is [YOUR NAME] from [COMPANY]. I know I'm calling out of the blue - do you have 30 seconds?"

*Wait for response*

> "I work with [ROLE] at companies like [REFERENCE CUSTOMER] who were struggling with [SPECIFIC PAIN]. We helped them [SPECIFIC OUTCOME].
>
> I'm not sure if that's relevant for you, but I thought it was worth a quick call to find out. Is [PAIN] something your team deals with?"

### Key Principles

1. **Acknowledge the interruption** - Shows self-awareness
2. **Ask for minimal time** - 30 seconds feels safe
3. **Lead with relevance** - Similar company, specific pain
4. **End with a question** - Invites dialogue

### Variations by Persona

**For Sales Leaders**:
> "...who were struggling with ramp time for new hires"

**For Enablement**:
> "...who were spending too much time on manual coaching"

**For RevOps**:
> "...who couldn't connect training to pipeline metrics"

### Handling Quick Objections

**"I'm busy"**
> "Totally understand. When would be better for a 5-minute call - Tuesday or Thursday morning?"

**"Send me an email"**
> "Happy to. So I send something relevant - what's your biggest priority this quarter for [area]?"

**"We're all set"**
> "Glad to hear it's not a burning issue. Mind if I ask what you're using today? Just so I know whether to follow up."

**"How'd you get my number?"**
> "LinkedIn/your website/referral from [person]. I should have mentioned - [NAME] suggested I reach out."

### Metrics to Track
- Connect rate (calls to conversations)
- Conversation to meeting rate
- Best time slots (track by hour)
- Opener variations that work
