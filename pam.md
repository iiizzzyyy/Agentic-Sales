## Frequently Asked Questions

### 1. Who is Pam?

Pam is Jam’s AI-powered, voice-first assistant designed specifically for field sales reps. It helps you handle admin tasks, prep for meetings, and capture customer interactions — all hands-free, while you stay focused on selling.

### 2. How does Pam work?

You interact with Pam just by speaking. Call in, ask a question, or give a command like:

- “What’s my next meeting?”
- “Log a visit with Dr. Müller.”
- “Send a thank-you email with the brochure.”

Pam listens, understands, and instantly updates your CRM, drafts emails, or navigates you to the next appointment. It integrates seamlessly with your existing tools (CRM, ERP, calendar).

### 3. How can Pam support field sales reps?

Pam makes your day in the field smoother by:

- **Planning & navigation**: reads your day plan, navigates to customers, reschedules if needed
- **Preparation**: briefs you on customer history, last orders, open tickets, and upsell opportunities
- **Post-meeting logging**: captures notes, follow-ups, and orders instantly by voice
- **Follow-up automation**: generates emails, books next steps, attaches documents
- **Performance insights**: answers “How’s my month going?” or “What’s my Q3 target?” in seconds

### 4. How much time does Pam save?

On average, reps using Pam save **4 hours per week** by cutting out repetitive admin work like CRM updates, writing follow-up emails, or manually entering orders. That’s time you can reinvest in customer conversations.

### 5. Is Pam replacing me as a field sales rep?

Not at all. Pam is **your assistant, not your replacement**. It takes care of admin tasks and routine documentation so you can spend more time with customers, build stronger relationships, and close more deals.

### 6. What benefit do I have as a field sales manager?

With Pam, managers get:

- More accurate and timely CRM data (no more missing call notes)
- Faster onboarding and training through best practice sharing
- Better visibility into pipeline health and rep performance
- A happier, more productive team spending more time selling, less time typing

### 7. Which CRMs does Pam integrate with?

Pam connects seamlessly with the most widely used CRM systems in field sales, including **Salesforce, Microsoft Dynamics 365, HubSpot, and SAP**.

Its open API also allows for **custom integrations** with industry-specific CRMs or ERP systems — so Pam fits into your existing workflow without disruption.
